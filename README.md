# 👁️ Gaze for Windows

**The Premium Dynamic Island Productivity Companion.**

Gaze is a minimalist, aesthetic productivity tool for Windows. It features a cute companion that follows your focus and a powerful Pomodoro timer hidden within a cinematic **Dynamic Island**.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🏝️ Dynamic Island
A beautiful, fluid UI that drops from the top of your screen. 
- **Ball-Drop Animation**: Triggers when your mouse touches the top-center.
- **Pill Expansion**: Smoothly stretches to reveal the companion and timer.
- **Contextual Controls**: Play, Pause, and Stop your focus sessions with ease.

### 🍅 Focus Timer
Stay productive with a built-in Pomodoro engine.
- Customizable Work/Break intervals.
- Aesthetic countdown display.
- System tray integration.

### 👁️ Interactive Companion
A minimalist set of eyes that brings life to your desktop.
- **Cursor Tracking**: The eyes follow your mouse movement.
- **Emotional Feedback**: Reacts to your timer status.
- **Customizable**: Toggle tracking, sounds, and periodic peeks.

---

## ⌨️ Shortcuts

| Action | Shortcut |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Toggle Timer** | `Ctrl + Alt + P` |
| **Show Island** | `Shift + Ctrl + P` |

---

## 🚀 Installation
1. Download `Gaze_Setup.exe` from the [Releases](https://github.com/AtharvaBari/Gaze-Windows/releases) page.
2. Run the installer and follow the wizard.
3. Hover your mouse at the top-center of your screen to reveal Gaze!
